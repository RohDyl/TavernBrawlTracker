⚡ Setup

Each player takes an edge. Deploy within 6" of your edge.
Place 3 Prize Tokens in the middle — at least 6" apart and 6" from each deployment zone.
Shuffle Gear deck. Each player draws cards equal to squad size including BB.
Both roll d20. Highest wins Turn 1 initiative. Reroll ties.
Alternate placing models. Initiative player places first.
🍺 The Table Is the Terrain. All objects count as Clutter (Scavenge) and Cover (+4 Defense). No terrain pieces needed.

🧠 Core Concepts

👊 Engaged

Within 1" of an enemy. Affects Retaliation and Fist Fights. Can be engaged with multiple enemies at once.

👁 Line of Sight

Trace a straight line attacker → target. Blocked by other models and terrain taller than the model.

🛡 Cover

Any object between attacker and defender from attacker's perspective. +4 Defense. Same object counts for Cover and Scavenge.

🔄 Turn Structure — 4 turns or until one squad is fully KO'd

1 — Order Phase
Return all model cards to upright.
Gain 1 Order Token per model on the table.
Place a Command Token coin beside your BB (if alive).
Draw 1 Gear card.
Bid for initiative (Turn 2+): Both bid 1 card face-down. Reveal. Highest tip wins. Both discarded. No bids = holder keeps. Tied = player who didn't have it last turn wins.
2 — Action Phase
Initiative player decides who activates first.
Players alternate activating models.
1 Order Token = activate 1 model. Rotate card sideways.
Each model activates once per turn (except via Command Token).
Play 1 free Order card per activation. Reactions & Free Actions: anytime.
Command Token: activates any model, even already activated (once per model per turn, unless The Colonel ability applies). Lost if BB KO'd.
Pass when no tokens or choose not to. Phase ends when both pass.
3 — End Phase
Any unengaged model within 1" of a Prize Token claims it: +5 pts, remove token.
Check for victory.
Multiple friendly models within 1" of same prize — controlling player chooses which claims it.

⚔️ Actions

Rush Amended
Move up to Speed → Fist Fight an engaged enemy. Declare only when an enemy can be reached after moving. If no enemy can be engaged, choose a different action.
⚠️ Provokes Retaliation from engaged enemies when moving.
Leg It
Move up to Speed twice. Each move may go in a different direction.
⚠️ Provokes Retaliation on each move while engaged.
Stand Your Ground
No movement. Fist Fight an engaged enemy. Scrappers roll +1 attack die when using this action.
Shove Through
Move up to Speed in a straight line. Path cannot cross another model or terrain taller than 1".
✦ Does not provoke Retaliation.
Scavenge
Must be within 1" of any Clutter. Draw 3 Gear cards, keep 1, discard the rest.

🥊 Combat Amended

Fist Fight (must be engaged — within 1")
ATTACK: Roll attack dice. Each die ≤ your Rank = 1 hit.
DEFENCE: Each defence die ≤ Defence value = blocks 1 damage independently. Multiple dice each block 1.
DAMAGE: Total hits minus total blocked.
⭐ Critical Hit — roll exactly your Rank

Automatic hit · +1 bonus damage · Defender does NOT roll against this die.

Natural 1 — that die fails

If your only die = total miss. Other dice in the same roll still resolve normally.

👥 Gang Up

Scrappers +1 attack die per friendly Scrapper within 4". Maximum +2 bonus dice. Applies to Retaliation too.

⚔️ Retaliation Amended

When engaged model moves away or plays a ranged card while engaged: every engaged enemy makes a free Fist Fight first. Resolves BEFORE the action. If provoking model KO'd → action cancelled. If KO'd during chain → all remaining retaliations cancelled.

Ranged Attacks: Only via Gear cards. Requires Line of Sight. Provokes Retaliation from engaged enemies.

🩸 Blood Tokens & Bloodied State

Losing vs Spending Blood

Losing — from attacks/card effects.

Spending — using a Desperate Act.

Both reduce count. At 0 → KO'd, removed from table.

Bloodied Threshold
  • Scrapper: 2 or fewer
  • Brute: 3 or fewer
  • BB: half starting Grit or below

BBs: flip card to Bloodied side permanently. All Bloodied Desperate Acts cost 1 less blood (min 1).

🎴 Gear Cards

Order

During your activation. One per activation at no extra cost.

Reaction

In response to a declared action. No Order cost. Either player may play.

Free Action

Any point during your turn. No Order cost.

⭐ Special Action

Timing is printed on the card — overrides normal timing rules. No Order cost unless stated. No archetype bonuses ever apply.

Archetype bonuses require your matching BB to be alive. Deck runs out → shuffle discard to reform. Common 33 · Uncommon 15 · Rare 8 · Special 6 · Total 62.

⭐ Special Cards — No archetype bonuses. Available to any squad.

🚪 The Bouncer ×2
Special Action — must be played as the FIRST action of the Action Phase, before any model activates.
Target opponent loses 1 Order Token this turn. If they have none, no effect.
"He's not paid to argue. He's paid to point at the door."
🍽️ Daily Special ×2
Special Action — may be played at any point during your turn.
Gain 1 Order Token immediately. May be spent to activate any friendly model — including one already activated this turn.
"Today's special: a second wind, served warm."
✂️ Cut Off ×2
Special Action — play when any player plays any Gear card, including another Cut Off.
That card is cancelled and discarded with no effect. Costs already paid are not refunded. A second Cut Off can cancel the first — chain continues until no further Cut Offs are played.
"Sorry mate, you've had enough."

💥 Desperate Acts — spend blood during activation or as a reaction. Min cost: 1 blood.

Come On Then!
Normal 1 blood Act. +1 attack die on next FF this activation.
Bloodied 1 blood → +2 attack dice instead.
Blind Fury
Normal 2 blood Act. Free FF against any engaged enemy outside your normal action.
Bloodied 1 blood → On hit, push target 2".
Get Out of My Way
Normal 1 blood React. Move up to 3" immediately. No Retaliation.
Bloodied 1 blood → Move up to 5" instead.
Dig Deep
Normal 3 blood Act. +2 attack dice AND +1 defence die for rest of this activation.
Bloodied 2 blood → Same effect.
Last Stand Amended
Normal 1 blood Act. Friendly within 4": when that model would next lose their last blood (from attack/card only, not spending), they keep 1. Once per game per model.
Bloodied 1 blood → Range: any friendly on table.
